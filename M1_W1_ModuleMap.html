WEEK 1 MAP
Welcome to Elsinore
This week you meet the play and its central puzzle, learn the sound of its verse, and write your first lines. Here is everything for Week 1 and the order to do it in.
🎯 THE QUESTION BEHIND MODULE 1
What does it mean to know yourself, and what does conscience demand when knowledge calls for action?
📋 DO THIS, IN THIS ORDER
1
Module 1 Opening Reflection (reflection, ungraded)
Give your first answer to the module question before we begin.
2
Lesson 1.01: Seeming and Being (literature 1 of 3)
Read Act I, scenes 1–2; meet the soliloquy and set up your Reading Journal.
3
Lesson 1.02: The Sound of the Soliloquy (literature 2 of 3)
Close-read Hamlet's first soliloquy and learn blank verse, the five-beat line.
4
Lesson 1.03: The Ghost's Charge (literature 3 of 3)
Finish Act I, scenes 3–5; study antithesis, the balanced pairing of opposites.
5
Lesson 1.04: Sentence Craft & Word Study (grammar + vocabulary)
Build stronger sentences with subordination; add two Act I words to your Journal.
6
Discussion: The Word "Seems" (graded, post + one reply)
Make one specific claim about Act I and answer a classmate.
7
Feeder 1: Twelve Lines of Trouble (graded, 10 pts, Word doc)
Write a short blank-verse soliloquy of your own.
🔄 RUNNING ALL MODULE (start now, do not turn in yet)
Reading Journal (Module 1 Organizer): add notes and Word Study each week; a Reading Journal Check comes later in the module.
Recitation thread: next week you choose the soliloquy you will memorize and perform in Week 7.
