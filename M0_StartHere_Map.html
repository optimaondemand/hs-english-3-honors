💬 ARE YOU ON-DEMAND OR LIVE?

These pages are written for On-Demand students, who work through the course independently on their own schedule. Everything you need to read, think, write, and submit is here, and a facilitator supports you along the way.

If you are a Live student, you will meet in person with your class in Microsoft Teams and in VR. Your teacher may adapt these On-Demand materials, move at a different pace, or assign different texts. When your teacher's instructions differ from a page here, follow your teacher.

📚 HOW THIS COURSE WORKS (ON-DEMAND)

This is a self-paced course you can complete independently. Each week follows the same rhythm: three literature lessons that read and analyze the text, one grammar and vocabulary lesson, a short discussion, and a writing assignment that builds toward the quarter's project. You keep a Reading Journal the whole quarter and add to it as you read.

You are never on your own. A live facilitator grades your work, answers email, and holds office hours; reach out whenever you are stuck. Some activities are recorded in a virtual (VR) space; those are always done solo, in a workspace you choose, and you submit a recording, so nothing depends on a particular headset or platform.

Open each week with its Module Map page. It lists everything to do, in order, and ends with a checklist so you can confirm you turned everything in.
