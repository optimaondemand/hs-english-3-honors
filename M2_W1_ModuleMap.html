WEEK 1 MAP
From the Stage to the Republic
Last quarter a prince asked how to govern himself. This quarter a new nation asks how a free people govern themselves together. You begin with the argument at the heart of the American founding: James Madison's Federalist No. 10. Here is everything for Week 1, in order.
🎯 THE QUESTION BEHIND MODULE 2
How do free people order their common life, how does a free person order an inner one, and what happens when a nation denies that self-government to some of its people?
