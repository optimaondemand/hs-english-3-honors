English III Honors — Independent Reading: Approved Book Menu

Choose one book per quarter from the categories below. Titles marked as course anchors are not available for independent choice in the quarter they are studied: Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass (Douglass) is the Quarter 2 anchor text, and Fahrenheit 451 (Bradbury) is the Quarter 4 anchor text.

Where a title says “any by author,” you may substitute another work by that same author (except where noted). See the Independent Reading page in Module 0 for the full log and quarterly check requirements.
