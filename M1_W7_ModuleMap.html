You have read the whole play. This week you pull it together into an argument, learn how your Module 1 project works, and perform the recitation you have been preparing all quarter. Here is Week 7, in order.
🎯 THE QUESTION BEHIND MODULE 1
What does it mean to know yourself, and what does conscience demand when knowledge calls for action?

1
Lesson 7.01: The Arc of the Examined Self (synthesis)

2

How the Elsinore Stage project works, and how to find a gap to fill.
3
Lesson 7.03: Speaking the Verse (elocution craft)

Perform your memorized soliloquy and submit the recording.
🔄 LOOKING AHEAD
Weeks 8 and 9 are your Elsinore Stage project: you will draft and then finalize an original lost scene or soliloquy for a secondary character. Today's Lesson 7.02 sets it up, so bring the ideas from your Week 4 Character Dossier. When you are ready to plan, open the Elsinore Stage Workshop page: a fillable tool that walks you from reflecting on your character through a beat outline to your opening lines, with a step for checking your rhythm with Claude and logging it.
